Complex numbers calculator, including interfaces.

1. Click 1 to perform the operation
Syntax:
operand complexNumber complexNumber
Example of a call:
+ 0+2i 3+2i

2. Click 2 to display the value of the mathematical variable
a) Click 1 to see the value of PI
b) Click 2 to see the value of E
c) Click 3 to see the value of the FI number
d) Click 1 to see the value of Euler's Constant

3. Click n to continue
4. Click y to exit the program
